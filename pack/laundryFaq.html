How much does it cost?

For standard, wash & fold service we charge a flat rate per bag. As much as you can fill! Included with the bag price are items needing special care; hand-wash, air-dry, stain treatment. We'll also hang and fold according to your preferences. Ironing is a separate fee of $3 per garment. Items that are not clothing (tablecloths, cleaning rags, etc.) are priced based on the quantity and other processing requirements. Contact our Sales Team for specialty pricing. View our pricing page for a list of all services and pricing (link for pricing page)

How do i find out if you cover my area?

On the Home page or the Residential page, enter your address in the "enter pickup address" box. We'll check to see if our drivers serve your location and let you know right away!

How do i get started?

Setting up laundry service is easy! Create your account on our website and place your first order!

How do i know how much wash & fold im sending?

Our wash and fold service is charged a fixed rate per 13-gallon bag, which is typically the same size as a kitchen trash bag. If you've sent your laundry in 13-gal bags, that's the number of bags you'll see on your receipt. We do have reusable bags ($6.00/ ea) for purchase. *If you send laundry in anything other than a 13-gallon bag, we'll measure and charge accordingly.

How do i pay?

Laundry Care uses a convenient, cashless payment system. Your payment information is stored on our secure server. When your laundry is dropped off, your credit card is charged for the amount of bags given and additional services requested (ironing * dry cleaning courier * rush * etc.). We accept Visa * Amex * Discover * Master Card.

When is my laundry returned?

Our standard turnaround is 2 days and is included in our service (no extra fee). If you need it back sooner you can request next day delivery. There is an additional mark up of 30% of your invoice for a faster turnaround.

How do i schedule a pick up?

You can schedule a pick up by creating an order via your account dashboard when signed into our site. #1) Sign in to your Account, #2) Click "Request a Pickup", #3) Create an order and select a day and time for pickup and delivery.

Can you dry clean items?

Dry Cleaning is unavailable at this time.

Can i get my garments ironed?

Yes! The cost for ironing is $3 per garment. Simply tell your Provider which item(s) need ironed and your starch preference. We'll press/steam according to your specifications.
